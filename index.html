Problem Statement: Household Organization

Our users struggle with household organization and cleanliness due to a lack of remembering to complete the needed chores. Our solution should provide users with reminders of when to start and complete their chores.

Affinity Diagram: Household Organization

Using the information discussed on our problem statement for the Household Organization app, my group and I expanded on the potential features that we want our app to includde to make it a more all-encompassing app for various aspects of household management.

Persona: 4 Personas for Household Organization App

Using the information discussed on our affinity diagram assignment for the Household Organization app, my group and I created personas for fictional individuals that would represent varying average users of our app to better understand the needs of our consumers.

Storyboard: 4 Storyboards for Household Organization App

Using the information discussed on our personas assignment for the Household Organization app, my group and I created storyboards for our own personas to demonstrate how our users might go about discovering and using our app.

Sketches: Potential Household Organization App's UI Design

Using the brainstormed ideas for the Household Organization app's visual design, my group and I individually generated sketches for various potential user interface looks.
